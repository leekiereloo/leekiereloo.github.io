## Description of Service

1000 Rejections is a personal productivity app designed to help you track and gamify rejection experiences. The App allows you to:

- Log "Asks" (requests you make to others)
- Track the status of your Asks (Idea, Asked, Rejected, Win)
- Monitor your progress toward a rejection goal
- Record reflections on your experiences
- Set daily reminders
- Export your data
